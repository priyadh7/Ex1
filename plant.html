Welcome to the World of Plant-Based Eating

A plant-based diet emphasizes whole, natural foods like vegetables, fruits, legumes, seeds, and grains while reducing or avoiding animal products. It's not only beneficial for your health but also for the planet.

Why Choose a Plant-Based Diet?

Health & Environmental Perks

  • Health Benefits
    • Lower cholesterol and blood pressure
    • Reduced risk of chronic diseases
  • Environmental Benefits
    • Decreased carbon footprint
    • Conservation of water and land

Simple Steps to Get Started

  1. Begin with One Plant Meal Daily
    1. Try oatmeal or smoothie for breakfast
    2. Add salads and veggie wraps for lunch
  2. Plan Weekly Meals
    1. Use plant-based recipe websites
    2. Do weekend prepping

Key Terms to Know

Vegan
Someone who avoids all animal-derived products, including dairy and eggs.
Flexitarian
A mostly vegetarian diet that occasionally includes meat or fish.
Whole Foods
Unprocessed or minimally processed foods without added chemicals.
